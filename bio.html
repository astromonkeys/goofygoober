About me

I am a fourth year student at UW-Madison studying Computer Sciences and pursuing a Studio Art certificate. My main areas of interest are human-computer interaction, improving the user experience, and making computing more accessible to all. I aspire for a technical career that truly helps others - ideally in software development - and am considering getting a masters degree in the future.

Outside of my academic/professional life - I am very into music, I am constantly listening to and discovering new music! I also play a variety of instruments(guitar, drums, etc.) and enjoy composing and producing. I love connecting with other people through music, it truly is a universal language and sparks joy wherever it's heard.

I am also a visual artist, primarily making work at the intersection of code and design. Most of my work is computer generated - produced by code. Typically, this takes shape as a hands-off algorithm where I determine the "rules" of the piece, and the code will produce outputs based on those rules - my art page has a few recent examples!

I also like to stay active, whether it be exploring Madison itself or going for a run, bike ride, skiing, or immersing myself in the nature nearby. Summer of 2022, I'm going on a 7-week backpacking trip through Europe after I graduate college, where I hope to discover other cultures and share the joy of music.
